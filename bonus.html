Valerie Dominguez

Valerie Domínguez was born on January 12, 1981 in Barranquilla, Colombia as Valerie Domínguez Tarud. She is an actress, known for Beyond Brotherhood (2017), Do You Believe? (2015) and El ultimo matrimonio feliz (2001).
